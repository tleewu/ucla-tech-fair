WELCOME TO UCLA'S 6TH ANNUAL TECH FAIR

Each year in October, the UCLA Residential Life hosts a Tech Fair, a campus-wide event where the top companies in technology come to showcase their innovative products to the students at UCLA. Because of technology, this generation can now connect with the world with a tap of a finger. The mission of Tech Fair serves to prepare students in their respective industries and update them on the tools they have at their disposal.
